# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

SentientWave Automata is committed to a respectful, harassment-free community experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Accept feedback with professionalism.
- Focus discussions on technical outcomes, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Threats, intimidation, or abusive language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive trolling or bad-faith engagement.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions/comments that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report violations, contact:
- conduct@sentientwave.com

All reports are reviewed and handled with reasonable confidentiality.
